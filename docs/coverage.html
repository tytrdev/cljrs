Callable values

Keywords, maps, sets, and vectors invoke like functions:

(:name {:name "ty"})        ;=> "ty"
({:a 1 :b 2} :a)            ;=> 1
(#{1 2 3} 2)                ;=> 2
([10 20 30] 1)              ;=> 20

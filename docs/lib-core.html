cljrs.core

The base namespace, mounted by install_prelude at startup. Roughly 125 host builtins (Rust, see src/builtins.rs) plus a small layer of cljrs-authored macros and seq helpers (see src/core.clj). It is a faithful subset of clojure.core — same names, same argument order, same nil-punning. str/* is also reachable as clojure.string/*.

Design goal: anything you reach for to write small Clojure should already exist. Anything that needs a JVM (reflection, proxy, agent) does not.

Arithmetic & predicates

  • (+ & xs) · (- & xs) · (* & xs) · (/ & xs) — variadic; promote to float on division or float arg.
  • (= a b ...) · (< a b ...) · (<= ...) · (> ...) · (>= ...)
  • (inc x) · (dec x) · (mod a b) · (rem a b) · (quot a b)
  • (min & xs) · (max & xs) · (abs x)
  • (zero? x) · (pos? x) · (neg? x) · (even? x) · (odd? x)
  • Type predicates: nil? string? number? integer? float? map? vector? set? list? seq? coll? keyword? symbol? fn? boolean? true? false? some?

Collections

  • (count c) · (empty? c) · (not-empty c)
  • (first c) · (rest c) · (second c) · (last c) · (nth c i)
  • (cons x c) · (conj c & xs) · (concat & cs)
  • (list & xs) · (vector & xs) · (vec c) · (set c) · (hash-map & kvs) · (hash-set & xs)
  • (get m k) · (get-in m ks) · (contains? m k) · (find m k)
  • (assoc m k v) · (assoc-in m ks v) · (dissoc m & ks) · (update m k f) · (update-in m ks f)
  • (keys m) · (vals m) · (merge & ms) · (select-keys m ks) · (zipmap ks vs) · (update-keys m f) · (update-vals m f)
  • (reverse c) · (sort c) · (distinct c) · (frequencies c) · (group-by f c)
  • Note: collections are persistent (imbl); mutation returns a new value, no transient yet.

Sequence ops (lazy + strict)

  • (map f c) · (filter pred c) · (remove pred c) · (reduce f c) · (reduce f init c) · (reduce-kv f init m)
  • (mapv f c) · (filterv pred c) — eager + return vector.
  • (mapcat f c) · (map-indexed f c) · (keep f c) · (keep-indexed f c)
  • (take n c) · (drop n c) · (take-while pred c) · (drop-while pred c) · (take-nth n c)
  • (partition n c) · (partition-all n c) · (interleave & cs) · (interpose sep c) · (dedupe c)
  • (range) · (range n) · (range a b) · (range a b step) · (repeat x) · (repeat n x) · (iterate f x) · (cycle c)
  • (seq c) · (flatten c) · (into to from) · (transduce xf f init c)
  • Gotcha: infinite seqs (iterate, cycle, repeatedly) require a bounded consumer like take — printing one will hang.

Functional helpers

  • (comp & fs) · (partial f & args) · (complement pred) · (juxt & fs) · (constantly x) · (identity x)
  • (apply f & args last-coll)
  • (some pred c) · (every? pred c) · (max-key f & xs) · (min-key f & xs)
  • (reduced x) · (reduced? x) · (unreduced x) — early-exit signal for reduce.

Strings & keywords

  • (str & xs) · (subs s a b?) · (name k) · (keyword s) · (symbol s)
  • str/split · str/join · str/upper-case · str/lower-case · str/replace · str/trim
  • str/starts-with? · str/ends-with? · str/includes? · str/blank? · str/index-of · str/last-index-of
  • All str/* are also bound at clojure.string/*.

Math

  • (sqrt x) · (pow b e) · (exp x) · (log x)
  • (sin x) · (cos x) · (tan x)
  • (floor x) · (ceil x) · (round x)
  • (int x) · (long x) · (double x) · (float x) · Math/PI

Atoms & state

  • (atom x) · (deref a) / @a · (reset! a x) · (swap! a f & args) · (compare-and-set! a old new) · (atom? x)
  • Note: no add-watch; the UI layer uses a thunk-based re-render pattern instead.

Errors & I/O

  • (throw x) · (ex-info msg data) · (ex-message e) · (ex-data e) · standard try/catch/finally.
  • (println & xs) · (print & xs) · (pr-str & xs) · (println-str & xs) · (print-str & xs)
  • (slurp path) · (spit path s) — native only; in wasm they raise.
  • (read-string s) — invoke the reader from data.

Regex

  • (re-pattern s) · (re-find re s) · (re-matches re s) · (re-seq re s)
  • Backed by Rust regex; PCRE-style features that aren't in regex (lookahead, backrefs) are not supported.

Macros & control flow

  • Threading: -> · ->> · some-> · some->> · cond-> · cond->> · as->
  • Conditionals: when · when-not · cond · if-let · when-let · and · or
  • Iteration: dotimes · doseq · for (with :when / :while / :let)
  • Records & protocols: defrecord · defprotocol — interpreted, no JVM-style polymorphism dispatch table.
  • Lazy seqs: lazy-seq · lazy-cat · realized? · force-seq
  • Misc: defn- (private fn) · gensym
